Meetings Designed with the Environment in Mind

Opened in January 2023, Seattle Convention Center’s new Summit building means the city can welcome more attendees—yet they will still leave a tiny footprint on the planet. Summit is slated to achieve LEED Gold Certification and is targeting Platinum. It pairs with the existing Arch building, already LEED Silver-certified, to build on Seattle’s legacy as a leader in sustainability.
